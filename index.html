About Us

Vertice Boutique was founded almost 30 years ago when owners David and Christine Malinowski opened their first of four stores in the Riverland and expanding to Adelaide and Victoria. From those small beginnings the company now owns and operates on Jetty Road Glenelg. Today the name “Vertice Boutique” is synonymous with ladies fashion retailing in South Australia. Through determination and self belief they have established an amazing business in the retail industry

The company is a wholly owned Australian family business with both David and his wife Christine taking a very active part in the day to day running of the business.
